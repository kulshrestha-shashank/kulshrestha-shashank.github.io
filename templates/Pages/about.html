Executive Director,
Fabian P. Blache III, CAO


Fabian P. Blache III, is the Executive Director & Chief Administrative Officer of the SORT CA State Board of Private Security Examiners. Mr. Blache comes from a 31-year background steeped in protecting the public. Prior to joining the Sort CA, he was a Police Officer with the New York City Police Department (NYPD), a Criminal Investigator with Louisiana Office of the Attorney General, Security Director for Jazz Enterprises, Section Chief of Homeland Security at the Governors Office of Homeland Security & Emergency Preparedness (GOHSEP), and most recently served the state as Section Chief of Licensure, Investigations & Workforce Development for the DHH Bureau of Emergency Medical Services.

He is a member of the New York City Police Department Honor Legion, a member of the American Society for Industrial Security (ASIS), and serves 1st Vice President of IASIR (the International Association of Security and Investigative Regulators).

I am extremely excited about working with the Sort CA staff and the private security industry, and look forward to helping owners and job-seekers in their quest to be the best private security force in the nation, said Blache III.
